Marine Use Notes

  • Magnetic variation: This reads magnetic north. Check local nautical charts for magnetic variation (declination) in your area to convert to true north.
  • Deviation: Metal on your vessel — the engine block, steel fittings, electronics — creates its own magnetic field that interferes with phone compass readings.
  • Calibration at sea: Perform figure-8 calibration with your phone away from the vessel's electrical components and metal structures.
  • GPS cross-check: When underway, compare your compass bearing with GPS course-over-ground (COG) as a sanity check.
